K Means is a method of finding clusters in arbitrary N-dimensional data. It works as follows:
  1. Randomly create K cluster centroids (N-dimensional poitns)
  2. Assign all points to the centroid they're nearest
  3. Re-position the centroids so they are at the geometric center of the points in their cluster
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until the assignment of points to clusters stops changing
